Why The Sensor Result Is Credible

The road test used fixed ultrasonic thresholds: 100 cm for far sensors and 100 cm for near sensors.

The false positives are measured from labelled real road samples, not guessed: 59 empty-road samples were detected as occupied.

The labels also include 1284 vehicle-present samples and 876 empty-road samples.

Reliability response now implemented in firmware: per-sensor threshold commands, median3 distance filtering, debounce2 occupancy state, and sensor-health WARN/FAIL diagnostics. Next field run should compare FP/FN with this filter enabled.

Detection Interpretation

The reported 96.6% accuracy comes from comparing the sensor state against manually labelled real crossroad observations.

False negative rate was 1.2%, meaning most vehicle-present samples were detected. False positive rate was 6.7%, which is the main target for future tuning.
